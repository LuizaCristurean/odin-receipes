Carbonara Pasta

Description

Carbonara is a classic Roman pasta dish made with spaghetti, eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, black pepper, and crispy pancetta. It's creamy without using any cream â€” the sauce forms from the heat of the pasta.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta or bacon
  • Egg Yolk
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, mix eggs, grated cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Drain the pasta, saving a bit of the cooking water.
  5. Mix the hot pasta with the pancetta, then add the egg mixture off the heat.
  6. Stir quickly to create a creamy sauce (add a little pasta water if needed).
  7. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
