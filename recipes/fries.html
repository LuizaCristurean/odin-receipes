Fries

Description

Fries (or French fries) are thin, stick-shaped pieces of potato that are deep-fried or baked until golden and crispy on the outside, soft on the inside. Often served with salt, ketchup, or mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Salt
  • Optional: paprika, garlic powder, herbs

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin strips.
  2. Soak them in cold water for 30 minutes (optional for extra crispiness).
  3. Dry the potatoes thoroughly with a towel.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan or fryer to 175°C (350°F).
  5. Fry the potatoes in batches until golden and crispy (5–7 minutes).
  6. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  7. Sprinkle with salt and optional seasonings.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite sauce.
