Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layered Italian pasta dish made with sheets of lasagna noodles, a rich meat and tomato sauce (ragù), creamy béchamel sauce, and grated cheese, all baked until golden and bubbling.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Grated mozzarella or parmesan
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil, then add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce to the meat and let it simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, béchamel sauce, and cheese.
  6. Repeat layers and finish with cheese on top.
  7. Bake for 30–40 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
